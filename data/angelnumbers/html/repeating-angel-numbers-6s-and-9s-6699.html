Repeating Angel Numbers - 6's and 9's (6699, 6669, 6969, 66699 etc)

A repetitive 6 and 9 combination indicates that it is time for you to detach from your material items, especially if you have had any obsession with any particular material possession. 

The 6 and 9 number combination asks you to 'let go and detach' to release the ‘old’.  This is also a message that something in your life is about to be replaced with something better.  Remember that you deserve the best.  Be open to receiving new possessions that exceed your expectations as you are ready for an upgrade in your life.
