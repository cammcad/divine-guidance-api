ANGEL NUMBER 1515


Number 1515 is a blend of the attributes and vibrations of number 1 and number 5, with both numbers appearing twice, amplifying their influences and energies.  Number 1 relates to new beginnings, striving forward and taking advantage of new opportunities, activity and assertiveness, tenacity, achievement and success.  Number 1 tells you that you create your own reality with your intentions, thoughts and beliefs and encourages you to step out of your comfort zone.  Number 5 resonates with the attributes of making life choices and important changes, adaptability and versatility, resourcefulness, adventure, motivation and idealism.  Number 5 also relates to doing things your own way and learning life lessons through experience.  

 Angel Number 1515 is an important message to do with changes in your life that will bring about auspicious new opportunities to enhance and enrich your life. Your thoughts, words and actions have an influence on the results and outcomes of all that takes place in your life, therefore maintain a positive attitude and use positive affirmations to ensure that all changes taking place and the ‘new’ entering your life will be most positive and life-improving.

 Be inspired to take action towards pursuing new opportunities appearing in your life at this time as they are appearing for you to take full advantage of and will be of great benefit to yourself and others. Take charge of your own life, do things your own way, and make positive choices and changes that suit who you truly are and what you truly want from your life.  Stay positive and optimistic about these impending changes, and you will find that all will go smoothly.

 Accept change in your life.  Call upon your higher-self and enjoy the challenges and experiences to be had. Go your own way in peace, love and joy.

 Number 1515 relates to number 3 (1+5+1+5=12, 1+2=3) and Angel Number 3.
