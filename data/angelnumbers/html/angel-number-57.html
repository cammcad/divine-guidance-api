ANGEL NUMBER 57


Number 57 is a combination of the energies and attributes of number 5 and number 7. Number 5 lends its vibrations of important life changes, adaptability and versatility, learning through experience, idealism and intelligence, and making positive life choices. Number 7 is the number of peace, spiritual awakening and enlightenment, faith, persistence of purpose, mysticism, empathic and psychic abilities, patience, inner-wisdom and understanding.

 Angel Number 57 is a message from your angels to confidently follow the promptings of your intuition and higher-self. Trust your inner-wisdom to lead and guide you through important life changes that will bring about positive outcomes on many levels.

Angel Number 57 is a message about making bold choices and changes in your life. It is time to focus on your personal spirituality and Divine life purpose and pursue your true soul mission without further delay. Call upon the angels for guidance and assistance if feeling any fears or doubts about your path. Be true to yourself and your personal choices and actions and trust your intuition, always.

Angel Number 57 tells of opportunities for you to expand your consciousness and spiritual awareness by meditating, taking time to go within, listening your intuition and following angelic guidance. Keep a strong, clear connection with your personal spirituality.
  

Number 57 also relates to number 3 (5+7=12, 1+2=3) and Angel Number 3.
