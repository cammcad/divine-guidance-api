ANGEL NUMBER 131


Number 131 is made up of the attributes of number 1 and number 3, with the vibrations of the number 1 doubled and amplified. Number 1 brings with it the attributes of self-leadership and assertiveness, initiative, instinct and intuition, new beginnings and a fresh approach. It also relates to motivation, striving forward and progress, and reminds us that we create our own realities by our thoughts, beliefs and actions. Number 3 resonates with energy, growth, expansion and the principles of increase, spontaneity, broad-minded thinking, self-expression, encouragement, assistance, talent and skills, manifesting and manifestation. Number 3 indicates that the Ascended Masters are around you, assisting when asked. The Ascended Masters help you to focus on the Divine spark within yourself and others, and assist with manifesting your desires. This makes 131 the number of optimism and enthusiasm, communication, creativity and expansion.

 Angel Number 131 is a sign that your angels are sending you positive and uplifting energies to clear away any feelings of discouragement, and to help keep your thoughts 
and beliefs optimistic and centred. Your angels ask you to stay focused on the true goals and aspirations of your soul.

 Angel Number 131 prompts you to use your natural skills and talents, and do not be afraid to step out of your comfort zones in order to pursue your true passion and life purpose.  We create our own realities with our beliefs, thoughts and actions, so make the choices and take action towards pursuing and living your life authentically. Trust that you have all the abilities within you, and when you take a step towards your true soul purpose, the angels and Universal Energies work with you to find success and fulfilment. Angel Number 131 encourages you to be true to yourself.

 Angel Number 131 is a message from your angels and the Ascended Masters that they are supporting and assisting with boosting your courage, resolve and self-confidence, so that you are able to powerfully manifest your wants, needs and desires. You are being offered guidance and advice about your soul mission, so pay attention to your intuition and inner-wisdom and take affirmative action as directed.

 Number 131 relates to number 5 (1+3+1=5) and Angel Number 5.
